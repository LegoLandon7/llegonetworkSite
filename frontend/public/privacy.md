# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 31, 2026  
**Effective URL:** [llegonetwork.dev/privacy](https://llegonetwork.dev/privacy)

At **llegonetwork.dev**, your privacy is our priority. We respect your information and are committed to transparency regarding how we collect, use, and store data when you interact with our Services.  

## Information We Collect
We collect only the minimum data required to operate our Services effectively. This may include Discord user IDs, server (guild) IDs, usernames, and basic profile information. Message content may only be accessed when required for bot triggers, commands, or functionality. We do **not** collect unnecessary personal data.  

## How We Use Information
Data collected is used solely for operational purposes. This includes responding to commands, processing bot triggers, maintaining functionality, improving performance, and debugging issues. We do not use data for advertising, tracking, or selling to third parties.  

## Minimal Data Retention
Data is stored temporarily and only as long as required for functionality or debugging purposes. Persistent storage is minimized, and any sensitive information is handled with care. Users have full control and may remove bots or request data deletion.  

## Open Source Transparency
Because parts of our Services are open source, the code that handles data is publicly viewable. We encourage users to review our repositories if they wish to understand how data is processed. Transparency is a key priority.  

## Third-Party Platforms
Our Services operate on third-party platforms, such as Discord. Data processed through these platforms is also subject to their privacy policies. **llegonetwork.dev** is not responsible for third-party handling of information.  

## User Control
You may stop using our Services at any time. Bots may be removed from your server to halt data collection. Where applicable, you may request deletion of any data we retain.  

## Security
We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection. Users are encouraged to avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily.  

## Children’s Privacy
Our Services are not intended for users under 13. If we discover data from a child, it will be promptly deleted.  

## No Selling or Sharing Data
We do **not** sell, rent, or trade user data. Data is handled solely for operational purposes within the Services.  

## Updates to Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Services signifies acceptance of the updated Policy. Users are encouraged to check [llegonetwork.dev/privacy](https://llegonetwork.dev/privacy) regularly.  

## Contact
If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding privacy or data handling, please contact us at [support@llegonetwork.dev](mailto:support@llegonetwork.dev).  

By using our Services, you acknowledge and accept that we collect only essential data, store it minimally, and prioritize transparency and user control at all times.
